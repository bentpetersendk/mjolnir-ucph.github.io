# Slurm FAQ

Q: Why is my job pending? A: Check pending reason, fairshare, resources, and partition/QoS limits.

Q: Can I run computation on the login node? A: No, use Slurm.

Q: How do I cancel a job? A: Use `scancel <jobid>`.
